About Me

The Eye
Behind the Lens

🎬
Sameer Singh · Karnal

I'm Sameer Singh, a self-built filmmaker and content creator from Karnal, Haryana. I started with nothing but a camera and a dream — and turned it into a creative practice that spans storytelling, editing, branding, and travel filmmaking.

My work lives at the intersection of cinematic craft and authentic emotion. Whether it's a travel film shot across the Himalayas or a brand story that needs to move people — I bring the same obsessive attention to every frame.

I teach advanced video editing on Instagram, work with international brands, and run Grace of God Organic — my family's agri-business. One person, many roles, one relentless drive.
